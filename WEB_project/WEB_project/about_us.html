We are AFK studios-Sofia

We are a "company" that sells musical instruments of all kinds, starting with Gibson, Shure, Yamahav , Steinway Musical Instruments and others.

Our teachers are selected, with many years of experience in pedagogues. Many of our students have participated in musical competitions and have won medals in international competitions.
